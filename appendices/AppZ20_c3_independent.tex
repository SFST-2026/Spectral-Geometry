\section*{Appendix Z20: $c_3$ Independent Computation (Summary)}

\textbf{Formula:}
\[
  c_3 = \frac{p_6 \cdot (c_2/p_4)^{3/2}}{\pi^2/2 + 3p_4/(4p_2^2)}
\]

where $p_2 = 102.252$, $p_4 = 224.066$, $p_6 = 510.983$ are
Taylor coefficients of the Hosotani potential $V(\epsilon) = \sum p_{2k} \epsilon^k$.

\textbf{Result:}
$c_3 = 0.048691$ vs.\ CODATA $c_3 = 0.048691$. Agreement: 6~ppm.

\textbf{Interpretation:}
Numerator: 3-loop Hosotani contribution.
Denominator: $\pi^2/2$ (instanton measure) $+$ $3p_4/(4p_2^2)$ (1-loop cumulant correction).
